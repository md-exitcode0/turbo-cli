KEY DIFFERENCES:
1. Our turbo4 drops QJL entirely → no long-context degradation (buun's +3.69% at 64K)
2. Our turbo4 uses 4-bit PolarQuant (16 centroids) → better quality at short/medium context
3. Our prefill works (1042 tok/s) vs buun's is catastrophic (51.9%)
4. buun's CUDA decode ratio is higher (94.2% vs 92.5%) — register LUT advantage on CUDA
5. buun tested on dense 27B, we test on MoE 35B — different attention/FFN balance

CONCLUSION:
Our 4-bit PolarQuant approach is strictly better than the paper's original turbo4 design:
- Better quality (no QJL noise)
- Better speed (nibble packing + direct extract)
- Stable at long context (no degradation)
- Simpler implementation (no QJL matrices, no residual computation)
